# ThinkBar — Project

## Purpose

ThinkBar is a macOS menu bar app that helps people capture thoughts and turn them into action. Prefer shipping small, useful increments over speculative features.

## Action First

Design and implement for doing, not browsing. Every screen, shortcut, and AI response should move the user toward a concrete next action.

## Swappable AI Provider

AI is a capability behind protocols. Never hardcode a single vendor into Core or Feature logic. Providers must be replaceable without rewriting product behavior.

## Daily Driver

Optimize for something people open every day on Mac: fast launch, low friction, keyboard-friendly, reliable in the menu bar / everyday workflow.

## Protect the MVP

Do not break existing MVP behavior for experimental work. Prefer additive changes, feature flags, or isolated modules. Refactors must keep current flows working.
